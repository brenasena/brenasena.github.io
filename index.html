About me

I have a passion for the intersection of the sciences and the arts. Having studied Biology and Public Health, I have always wanted to improve my coding skills to leverage this into my biological work. I believe that being able to visually represent biological processes, we will be able to better tackle the biggest challenges in biological breakthroughs.

In this way I hope to leverage my coding skills to creatively represent biology.
